## Key Insights 
   
   Correlation Summary
   - Sales & Profit: Strong positive correlation → Higher sales lead to higher profits
   - Quantity & Sales: Very weak correlation → More units sold ≠ More revenue
   - Quantity & Profit: Very weak correlation → High volume may have low margins
   - Insight: Focus on high-value products to increase profitability.

   - This correlation shows the strength of relationships between Quantity, Sales, and Profit.        There is a strong positive correlation between Sales and Profit, which means that as sales       increase, profit also increases. However, the correlation between Quantity and Sales, as         well as Quantity and Profit, is very weak. This indicates that selling more units does not       guarantee higher revenue or profit suggesting that some products may sell in high    quantities but at low margins.
